# Production Data Masking Framework with Business Continuity Compliance

## Project Overview
**Client:** Wells Fargo | **Duration:** 2021-2023 | **Role:** Senior Data Engineer

Engineered resilient production data masking framework with built-in Business Continuity Plan compliance, reducing processing time from 8 hours to 1 hour while achieving zero failures in production environment.

## Business Impact and Key Results
- **87.5% reduction** in processing time (8 hours to 1 hour)
- **100% BCP compliance** achieved with fault-tolerant design
- **Zero production failures** post-implementation
- **4-hour recovery time** reduced to 5 minutes through checkpoint system
- **100% data accuracy** maintained throughout masking process
