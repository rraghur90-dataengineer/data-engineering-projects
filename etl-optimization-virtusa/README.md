# Enterprise ETL Performance Optimization and Workflow Tuning

## Project Overview
**Client:** Virtusa | **Duration:** 2012-2019 | **Role:** Consultant to Senior Roles

Led comprehensive ETL performance optimization initiative, reducing critical business process runtime from 22 hours to 4 hours while achieving 100% SLA compliance and significant resource utilization improvements.

## Business Impact and Key Results
- **82% improvement** in ETL runtime (22 hours to 4 hours)
- **100% SLA compliance** achieved for business reporting
- **40% reduction** in resource utilization and infrastructure costs
- **Daily business reporting** enabled instead of weekly cycles
- **Zero performance-related** incidents post-optimization
